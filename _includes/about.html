Hello friends! My name is Laura Beth. I've lived in Nashville now for almost 5 years. I love great food and I love sharing greatness with others!

I'm a Leo as zodiac signs go. I'm light hearted, calm, hardworking, loyal, passionate, and independent. I seek balance in all things and am always looking to evolve and better my life experience.

Ladder & Dolly is the result of many years of brainstorming but primarily came to me during the postpartum recovery of my first son, in January 2014. I had a beautifully supportive and overwhelmingly gracious support team that hardly left my side, making sure I had water, organic snacks and healthy, well-rounded meals 24-7. This was an extremely beneficial way for me to gain back my strength and begin the incredible journey into motherhood. I am forever grateful to them for loving me this way. That said, it was a lot of work keeping foods prepared and at the ready!

This all sparked desire and inspiration within me to create a business where my focus is to enhance others' lives by providing service that ensures quality and convenience to each individual's needs and lifestyle.

Whether you are a new mother, a small family that's on the go, or you're just looking for help with meal planning and execution to support your already healthy and robust lifestyle, I can help!

"What's it all about?"

"What does the name mean?"

"Where did the name come from?"

The two words jumped off the screen at me one day in an email to the maintenance man of my apartment complex.

"... could you please leave me the ladder and dolly outside unit #369?"

The words not only rolled out of my mouth nicely as I read aloud, they also made me feel something. A ladder and a dolly ... as pieces of metal and rubber, expertly fashioned to be the tools that they are, they would soon be helping me set up a new home. Helping me with some of the heavy lifting, the hanging of curtains at the tippie top of 18 foot ceilings and providing me the ability to playfully move things about my space until it all felt just right.

Symbolically, I feel as though the name represents my passion in life. To help others obtain success and inspiration in their lives by sharing my own high standards for nutrition, health, and overall well being. To create more time, space, and ease for you and your family by preparing great quality foods and balanced meals that can be enjoyed together around a dining room table or on the go as you sprint out the door to work. To hopefully inspire and become inspired by those around me daily so that we're all feeling more and more playful about our time here together.
